# Financial Disclaimer

## Not Financial Advice

OpenClaw Fleet is an **open-source AI agent platform**. When configured with financial intelligence agents, it provides **informational market commentary and technical pattern analysis only**.

**It does NOT provide:**
- Financial advice
- Investment recommendations
- Trading instructions
- Guaranteed outcomes
- Professional portfolio management

## User Responsibility

- All outputs are **opinions generated by AI models** — they can be wrong, delayed, or based on incomplete data
- Users must **verify all data independently** before making any decision
- Users accept **full responsibility** for their own trading and investment decisions
- Past patterns, technical analysis, and historical analogs **do not predict future results**
- AI confidence scores are **calibration estimates, not guarantees**

## Data Limitations

- Market data accessed through web search may be **delayed 15–20 minutes or more**
- Real-time data requires paid data feeds not included in this project
- Technical analysis is performed on potentially stale data — always verify with your broker
- News and event data may be incomplete or lag behind real developments

## Risk Warning

Trading and investing involve **substantial risk of loss**. You can lose some or all of your capital. Do not trade with money you cannot afford to lose.

- Options and leveraged instruments carry additional risk
- Short selling has theoretically unlimited loss potential
- Low-liquidity instruments may not execute at expected prices
- System errors, delays, or outages can result in missed signals or stale alerts
